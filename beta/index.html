MineDraft

MineDraft allows you to draft your MineCraft ideas.

Have a cool new idea you want to share with your friends? Draw it in MineDraft and share the link with your friends. No need to log into Minecraft and take screenshots or videos and no uploading to file sharing sites.

Just click a block and start drafting!

Help

To use:

  1. Drag a block from the toolbar on the left.
  2. Drop it into the grid.
  3. Repeat Step 1.
  4. ...
  5. Profit.

Double-Click an object in the grid to delete it.
